### **4｜Demystifying AI: The Black Box Belongs to Everyone**

Did you know?

Even the creators at OpenAI can’t precisely predict what their models will output.
Large Language Models (LLMs) work through **probabilistic generation** across billions of parameters in high-dimensional space.
Tweaking a single value can’t guarantee the next word.

So yes—**AI is, and may always be, a black box.**

Current tuning methods (Fine-tuning, RLHF) remain empirical—trial, error, and observation.
There is no strict engineering control or mathematically rigorous execution model.
